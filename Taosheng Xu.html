Taosheng Xu

Dr. Taosheng Xu

Institute of Intelligent Machines, Chinese Academy of Sciences (CAS), Hefei, China

Email: taosheng.x@gmail.com


Dr. Taosheng Xu is currently an assistant research fellow at Institute of Intelligent Machines, Chinese Academy of Sciences ( CAS ). From Jan 2015 to Feb 2016, he worked with Prof. Jiuyong Li and Dr. Thuc Duy Le as a visiting PhD student in the University of South Australia, Australia.

Dr. Xu received his PhD degree in Pattern Recognition and Intelligent System from the University of Science and Technology of China ( USTC ) in Nov 2016.

Research Interests

Bioinformatics, Machine Learning, Precision agriculture
